Jon

I was born in China in the year 1997. My family moved to Berlin during the year 2000. I grew up in a very adventurous household. During my studies, I had a lot of fun working as a tour guide for different agencies.

Historical overview or detailed knowledge of a specific era, Fine arts or graffiti, 100-year-old ballroom or underground techno party – I will find the topics that you’re interested in. I will also make you proposals and suggestions according to your interests, to guarantee you a good time.
