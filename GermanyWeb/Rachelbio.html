Rachel

Berlin has many faces. I came here for art and I am dealing with art all the time. Music, concerts, exhibition and Street Art. I am a musician actually but street art is running after me whatever I go and here in Berlin I made research and started to lead tours after few months I moved.

I love to meet people from all around the world, share my opinion and discover through their stories the street art scene of their countries. I offer a social tour, made to have fun, discover Berlin and have nice time together.
