Frequently Asked Questions

Home About Us FAQs Tours Login
1. What is a tour?
  • A tour is a trip that people experience different places that ties into their vacation.
 
2. How do I sign up for a tour and how much is the deposit?
  • You can sign up for a tour on our website through the login option. If you have a login username/password then sign in. If this is the first time visiting our website, please create a username and password to get started today booking your tour!
 
3. How much is the deposit?
  • The deposit depends on the extent of the tour you are attending.

    • 1 tour: $100

    2 tours: $150

    3 tours: $175

    4 tours: $250

 
4. What is the cancellation policy?
  • Before or on 30 days, you will receive your full refund back. After 30 days, you will lose your deposit.
 
5. Do the tours ever get cancelled for weather?
  • No, the tour will still go on regardless of the weather. Our tours are rain or shine!
  • Comfortable walking shoes are recommended.
 
6. How long are the tours?
  • The tours are approximately 1-2 hours and about 1 mile for each tour.
 
7. What happens if we arrive late to the tour?
  • Team 4 Tours encourage you to arrive at least 10 minutes before the tour in order to not mess up the people on the tour that arrived on time. The lateness could affect your participation on the tour.
 
8. What type of payments methods do you accept.
  • Team 4 Tours accept credit/debit card payments (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), Venmo and PayPal.
 
9. Should I include airfare?
  • Team 4 Tours does not include airfare. Airfare is a separate purchase from all tours.
 
10. Can I request special dietary meals?
  • Yes, Team 4 Tours requires you to request special dietary meals before you attend the tour.
