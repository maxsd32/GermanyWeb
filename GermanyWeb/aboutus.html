ABOUT US

OUR MISSION

At Global Insight Germany, we come into work each and everyday with the goal of providing the most informative, interesting, and interactive tours in all of Germany at a reasonable and affordable price!


WHO ARE WE?

To put it bluntly, we are Global Insight Germany, a tour company dedicated to servicing tourists all over Germany. We were founded in late 2019 by a group of ambitious college students in Berlin, Germany with the goal of providing the best tours in all of the country. We made it a mission to put the customer first in our business and, rather than just give the exact same tour over and over, we instead take the approach of handcrafting and taloring every single tour we give around the specific wants and interests of the customer. In fact, almost every image you'll see on our website has actually been taken by our customers on their tours. Over just our first years, we have managed to grow exponentially, now having opened offices in Hamburg, Munich, Frankfurt, Leipzig, and Nuremburg and currently planning offices in both Essen and Dortmund by next year. We also are in the works of discussing bringing our company to other European countries such as France and Poland in order to bring the Global Insight Germany experience abroad.
